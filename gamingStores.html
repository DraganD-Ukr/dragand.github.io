TOP GAMING COMPUTER BRANDS

If you want to buy a gaming PC, you might go with a well-known brand known for its performance and quality. With a few of the best pre-built gaming PC brands, you can be sure that all your PC components would adequately work together, and you'll be covered by a lengthy warranty period if anything goes wrong.Branded gaming PCs are available at various price points, so there's always one to fit almost any budget and to quench your thirst.If you've been thinking: what are the best gaming PC brands that you could trust to get good gaming PCs from? This list contains the finest, top 10 gaming PC brands, frequently subsidiaries or divisions of well-known PC manufacturers.

1. OMEN

Omen is a highly rated tech company that began very small and was known as Voodoo PC before being acquired by HP. Voodoo PC was founded in Calgary, Canada, and produced luxury PCs and gaming systems. HP acquired the Voodoo brand in 2006 and continued to use the name for their gaming division until 2013. Since then, the company has been renamed Omen, but the original logo remains. HP is known for producing high-quality computer products. The Omen division uses resources and technology to create some of the greatest gaming PCs available. Omen now includes mice, headsets, and keyboards in the company's characteristic black with red accents color scheme. Its current top-of-the-line PC, the Omen Obelisk, has outstanding performance. This is the best brand to get the best gaming PC on a budget, no matter where you are in the world, and it perfectly answers the question: what are the best gaming PC brands?

2. ALIENWARE

Alienware began as Sakai in 1996 before changing its name to Alienware in 1997. Nelson Gonzalez, the company's founder, began by upgrading his friends' computers for gaming because regular PCs at the time were inadequate. Soon after, the company focused solely on gaming PCs, and its reputation flourished. The Alienware Blade, which earned the PC gamer editor's choice award, put the company on the map. Dell acquired Alienware in 2006, and the company has since continued to produce high-quality gaming hardware. The company is known for pushing the limits of what a gaming computer can be. Many of its designs are unique to the market, but the quality is always excellent. Alienware is still at the top of its game for gaming PCs, as evidenced by the new Aurora R11 Gaming Desktop. This is undoubtedly one of the best gaming PC brands. But it comes second after Omen due to their higher prices.

3. CORSAIR

In the year 1994, Corsair was founded in the state of California. Cache and DRAM modules were among the first computer components it produced. The company grew from humble beginnings to produce additional computer components before producing its own branded PCs. Corsair has continued to expand over time, acquiring companies such as Scuf Gaming, Origin Computers, and Elgato along the way. Corsair's ecosystem has grown to include everything from headsets and keyboards to cases and gaming PCs. Corsair products may all be linked via the ICUE software, one of the biggest reasons to utilize them. The software provides real-time status for all of your components, allowing you to sync them to function better together and have matching RGB lighting colors. The Corsair Vengeance i7200 is the company's latest gaming PC that performs like a full-time gaming machine. Although the Corsair Vengeance i7200 is not as expensive as some of its competitors, it still provides access to some of the most powerful hardware available. Because the Vengeance i7200 comes in a plain-looking casing with no peripherals, this is the case. However, if you value substance over style, this is the gaming PC for you. You can build a beast of a machine with CPUs up to an Intel Core i9-10850K and GPUs up to an Nvidia GeForce RTX 3090 if you can afford it.

4. REPUBLIC OF GAMERS (ROG)

Does this brand ring a bell to you? We're pretty sure it doesn't. This is because the Republic of Gamers (ROG) is the gaming division of the tech giants, Asus. ROG is a unique brand among the top 10 gaming PC brands. For example, Omen and Alienware rely on third-party manufacturers for some of their components. However, the ROG brand primarily makes use of in-house components to create excellent gaming hardware. If you don't want to buy a prebuilt PC, you can create a bespoke rig utilizing only Asus ROG components. ROG's latest gaming masterpiece is the Huracan G21CX. With just one tryout, you'll see that the ROG Huracan is unlike any other gaming desktop. Its asymmetrically designed chassis features a folding magnetic side cover that enhances airflow into the system and allows for a CPU and GPU performance boost when opened. The additional airflow offers the necessary cooling to unleash the full power of its 8th Generation Intel® Core TM i7 processor and NVIDIA® GeForce® GTX 1080 graphics for incredible gaming experiences – allowing you to play the latest games at their most intense settings. This makes this brand one of the best gaming PC brands for getting the best gaming PC custom build.

5. PREDATOR

Predator is a division of Acer that specializes in gaming hardware. Acer, which was formed in 1976, is a Taiwanese firm. It began by manufacturing components before transitioning to IBM PC compatible systems in the 1980s. Since then, it has expanded to become one of the world's foremost providers of PCs, laptops, and monitors. With the release of the Acer Aspire Predator Desktop PCs in 2008, the Predator brand was born. Laptops, monitors, and other accessories have all been added to the line. The Predator line's goods are of the greatest quality, and the predator Helios 700 is the most recent addition. The Predator Helios 700 features a sharply slanted chassis and an all-black color scheme, typical of Acer's Predator line. Its bold keyboard typeface and futuristic-looking power button are all part of the DNA. It's a look you have to enjoy if you want to buy a notebook like this one. We applaud Acer for being prepared to go further than many other brands to bring one of the best gaming PC deals in the market.

6. ORIGIN PC

Origin PC is currently a Corsair subsidiary. However, we've kept it on the list since it provides a distinct service from its parent company. Origin PC was founded in 2009 by former Alienware employees who wished to return to custom PC development. Corsair purchased the company in 2019 after it had grown at an exponential rate. Every device created by Origin PC is designed to your exact specs and requirements, which sets it apart from the other brands on this list and made it part of our good gaming PC brands recommended. Rather than spending hours assembling a one-of-a-kind rig, you can hire Origin PC to do it for you. Everything about the case, from the color to the material used for the panels, can be customized. You should expect to pay more for an Origin PC build than you would for a conventional prebuilt PC, but it's well worth it for a one-of-a-kind machine.

7. MSI

Micro-Star International (MSI) was founded in Taiwan in 1986. It's another firm that began by manufacturing motherboards and add-on cards. The company's expansion has been powered by its goods, which have all performed admirably. In 2004, it debuted its first notebook computer, and in 2009, it delivered its first all-in-one computer. MSI is well-known for its involvement in eSports, having started its gaming line by supporting FNATIC in 2008. MSI manufactures a wide range of products, including motherboards, graphics cards, and gaming cases. The company's gaming line includes laptops and desktops with various performance levels. You can choose between a budget-friendly Bravo Series laptop and a high-end Titan Series laptop. The Aegis Ti5 is the company's top-of-the-line gaming computer. MEG Aegis Ti5 features a 10th generation Intel® Core TM i9 processor with 10 cores, 20 threads, and a clock speed of up to 5.3GHz. MEG Aegis Ti5 can generate high frame rates for your games even under intense multitasking workloads. Its qualifying functions rightly place the MSI brand as one of the best gaming computer brands.

8. INTEL

Intel's technology has been at the center of computing innovations since its founding in 1968. They are a market leader, developing game-changing technology that promotes global progress and improves people's lives. Artificial intelligence (AI), the 5G network revolution, and the advent of the intelligent edge are all technical inflection points that will impact the future of technology. These are driven by silicon and software, and Intel is at the center of it all. Intel is well known for producing office or work computers, but what if we tell you that Intel has started setting off their gaming PCs which are also beginner-friendly. The IBUYPOWER element MR 9320 is an example of this hypothesis. The IBUYPOWER Element MR 9320 is one of the best prebuilt gaming PC on Amazon, which puts intel as one of the best prebuilt gaming PC brands around. The IBUYPOWER Element MR 9320 features a 10th-generation Intel Core i7-10700F processor, Nvidia GTX 1660 TI (6GB) graphics card, 16GB DDR4 RAM, a 240GB SSD, and a 1TB hard drive to store all of your games and pretty much everything else. These are respectable specs these days, and they're more than capable of running recent PC games at 1080p without a hitch.

9. MAINGEAR

MAINGEAR began in 2002 when Wallace Santos decided whether to pursue his interest in constructing high-performance computers or modding and tuning automobiles. Finally, he blended the best of both worlds by creating high-performance bespoke PCs and fine-tuning them for the greatest possible experience. Wallace has always prioritized providing the highest level of personal service, construction quality, and performance. MAINGEAR's devices have won various industry awards since 2002, including the Editors' Choice award from PC Magazine and the Editors' Choice award from Maximum PC Kick-Ass. MAINGEAR's commitment to true one-on-one personal customer service is a cornerstone of their business, distinguishing them from other PC manufacturers and custom PC builders. MAINGEAR is a privately held, self-funded firm reshaping the PC industry by developing new and better solutions while maintaining a commitment to providing exceptional service to its clients. You can count on MAINGEAR to give the best option when you need high performance, superior build quality, and award-winning service. MAINGEAR provides you with the peace of mind that only comes from an exceptional experience backed by cutting-edge technology. A MAINGEAR PC would be simply described as the best PC for a beginner. Its level of simplicity follows that of the intel brand but with slightly more added features. If you are looking for a high-performance custom PC with exceptional build quality and award-winning support, go no further because MAINGEAR has covered you. It's undoubtedly part of the few good gaming PC brands out there. It meets all your performance and quality requirements by combining cutting-edge technology with the expertise of a dedicated and personal support team, which makes this brand one of the best gaming PC brands available in the market.

10. NVIDIA'S GEFORCE

Nvidia's GeForce is one of the top gaming PC brands that creates graphic processing units (GPUs) and great gaming PCs. With GeForce RTX pc5 being the latest PC release, there have been seventeen revisions of the design concerning the GeForce RTX pc5 series. The earliest GeForce products were discrete GPUs for add-on graphics boards aimed at the high-margin PC gaming market. Later product diversification included all tiers of the PC graphics market, from cost-sensitive GPUs incorporated on motherboards to mainstream add-in retail boards. Nvidia's series of embedded application processors, developed for electronic handhelds and mobile handsets, has recently been included in GeForce technology. Only NVIDIA's RTX technology has dedicated ray tracing and AI tensor cores, allowing for breakthrough technologies at breakthrough speeds to enjoy today's biggest blockbusters as you have never seen them before.
